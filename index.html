Projects

Field research, spatial analysis, and conservation mapping. Expanding progressively.

2022–2024 Global Conservation Threat Mapping Earth Insight · WCPA / IUCN

Spatial analysis of fossil fuel, mining, and industrial expansion threats to protected areas, key biodiversity areas, and tropical forests across 100+ countries. Multiple peer reports and datasets used in international conservation policy.

2019–2020 Bolivia WATCH: Fire and Water Analysis SEI / USAID

Multi-scale hydro-ecological analysis of the 2019 Chiquitania fires; combined remote sensing, hydrological modelling, and field data. SEI Technical Report, 64 pp.

2015–2017 Vjosa River Hydro-ecological Assessment UNDP Albania / EU-FPIP

Assessment of hydro-ecological and socio-economic systems of the Vjosa River, Albania. Climate-change and development scenario modelling. UNDP Technical Report, 114 pp.

2004–2006 Mexico National Forest Fire Detection System GIZ / CONABIO · Mexico City

Developed and implemented Mexico’s satellite-based near-real-time forest fire detection system (MODIS/AVHRR). Capacity building with Mexican and Central American government partners.
